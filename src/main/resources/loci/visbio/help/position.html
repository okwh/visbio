Position settings

Being able to quickly and easily switch between dimensional positions is vital for proper data analysis. VisBio shows the current dimensional position at the bottom of the display window's controls, with a series of sliders available for roaming data quickly. Each slider corresponds to one of your dataset's dimensional axes. Together, they specify the dimensional position of the currently displayed image (or image stack).

Directly above the sliders, the "Animate" button cycles through the selected animation axis (specified in the drop-down box to the right of the button), using low-resolution thumbnails. The "FPS" selector allows specification of animation speed in frames per second.

Above the animation controls, you can choose which axes are traversed when the keyboard arrow keys are pressed. By default, left and right will roam through time, while up and down will step across focal planes.
