About VisBio


v3.00 beta3

VisBio was written by Curtis Rueden of the Laboratory for Optical and Computational Instrumentation (LOCI), White Laboratory of the Laboratory of Molecular Biology, University of Wisconsin-Madison, with contributions from Eric Kjellman.

Thanks to John White, Kevin Eliceiri, Jayne Squirrell, Bill Hibbard, Bill Mohler, Ilya Goldberg, Dennis Fan and Gerard Ziemski for ideas and assistance.

  •  
    		•  VisBio displays data with VisAD, a Java component library for interactive and collaborative visualization and analysis of numerical data.
  •  
    		•  VisBio includes ImageJ, an application for image processing and analysis in Java, and contains some ImageJ-related features.
  •  
    		•  VisBio includes functionality related to the Open Microscopy Environment (OME).
