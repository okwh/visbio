TIFF stacks

VisBio supports reading uncompressed and LZW-compressed TIFF stacks (not including metadata). Support is completely native, with no additional libraries required.

VisBio also supports writing TIFF stacks using ImageJ (which comes packaged with VisBio). In addition, VisBio can take advantage of the Java Advanced Imaging package should it be installed, enabling support for a wider variety of TIFF features.
