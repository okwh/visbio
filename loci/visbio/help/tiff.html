TIFF stacks

VisBio supports reading uncompressed and LZW-compressed TIFF stacks, including metadata. Support is completely native, with no additional libraries required.

For certain kinds of TIFF, VisBio utilizes the ImageJ library (packaged with VisBio). In addition, VisBio can take advantage of the Java Advanced Imaging package should it be installed, enabling support for reading a wider variety of TIFF features.

VisBio also supports writing uncompressed and LZW-compressed TIFF stacks natively plane by plane, to minimize memory usage.
