Zeiss ZVI (Axiovision)

VisBio supports reading some types of Zeiss Axiovision (ZVI) files (not including metadata). Support is completely native, with no additional libraries required.

ZVI support was added through trial and error (i.e., reverse engineering several example ZVI files), and does not work with some types of ZVI. It has been tested to work with 8-bit image stacks, 16-bit single images and 24-bit color images, and should work with 16-bit stacks and 24-bit color stacks as well.
